# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies manage customer relationships, sales, communication, and business workflows efficiently.

CRM systems help businesses:
- Store customer information
- Track interactions
- Improve customer relationships
- Increase sales efficiency

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies worldwide.

Businesses use Salesforce to:
- Manage customer data
- Track sales opportunities
- Automate workflows
- Improve customer service
- Analyze business performance

Salesforce helps businesses work more efficiently and make better decisions.

---

## Salesforce Concepts

### Account
An Account represents an organization or business.

Example:
A college or company.

### Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an Account.

Example:
A student or employee.

### Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a possible business deal.

Example:
A student likely to enroll in a college.

---

## Business Workflow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

### Lead
A person interested in a product or service.

### Contact
A known person whose details are collected.

### Opportunity
A potential business deal being discussed.

### Customer
A successfully completed deal.
